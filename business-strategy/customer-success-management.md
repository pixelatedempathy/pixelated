# Pixelated Empathy - Customer Success Management System

## Overview
This comprehensive customer success management system ensures exceptional client experiences, drives measurable outcomes, and builds long-term institutional partnerships for sustained growth and market leadership.

## Customer Success Team Structure

### Tier 1: Customer Success Managers (CSMs)
**Role Overview**: Strategic relationship managers for institutional accounts
**Account Load**: 15-20 institutional customers per CSM
**Reporting**: Director of Customer Success

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Quarterly business reviews with institutional leadership
- Expansion planning and upsell opportunities
- Customer health monitoring and risk mitigation
- Reference development and case study creation

**Skills & Qualifications:**
- Master's degree in psychology, social work, or related field
- 5+ years experience in healthcare/education customer success
- Strong understanding of mental health training and education
- Excellent relationship building and communication skills

### Tier 2: Customer Success Specialists
**Role Overview**: Tactical support and day-to-day account management
**Account Load**: 30-50 customers per specialist
**Reporting**: Customer Success Manager

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Weekly check-ins with program coordinators
- Technical troubleshooting and support escalation
- Training delivery and user onboarding
- Usage analytics and engagement monitoring

**Skills & Qualifications:**
- Bachelor's degree in psychology, education, or related field
- 2+ years experience in customer support or training
- Technical aptitude for platform support
- Strong organizational and communication skills

### Tier 3: Implementation Specialists
**Role Overview**: Technical setup, integration, and launch support
**Account Load**: 10-15 new implementations per month
**Reporting**: Director of Implementation

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Technical setup and system integration
- Custom configuration and LMS integration
- Initial training and user onboarding
- Go-live support and optimization

## Customer Journey Mapping

### Pre-Sales Phase
**Customer Success Involvement:**
- Technical demonstrations and pilot planning
- Customization requirements gathering
- Success criteria definition and measurement planning
- Reference customer introductions

**Deliverables:**
- Success plan and measurement framework
- Technical integration requirements
- Pilot program structure and timeline
- Risk assessment and mitigation strategies

### Onboarding Phase (Weeks 1-4)
**Week 1: Executive Alignment**
- Executive kickoff meeting with institutional leadership
- Success criteria confirmation and baseline establishment
- Technical setup and integration completion
- Communication plan and escalation procedures

**Week 2: Technical Setup**
- System configuration and customization
- LMS integration and single sign-on setup
- User account creation and permission management
- Initial testing and quality assurance

**Week 3: Training & Launch**
- Faculty/staff training sessions (4-hour comprehensive)
- Student/trainee onboarding (1-hour orientation)
- Initial scenario deployment and testing
- Usage monitoring and early optimization

**Week 4: Stabilization**
- Usage analytics review and optimization
- User feedback collection and initial adjustments
- Support process refinement and escalation procedures
- Success milestone confirmation and planning

### Adoption Phase (Months 2-3)
**Monthly Activities:**
- Usage analytics review and optimization recommendations
- User feedback sessions and program adjustments
- Faculty development workshops and advanced training
- Success story development and documentation

**Key Metrics Monitoring:**
- User engagement and completion rates
- Diagnostic accuracy improvement tracking
- User satisfaction and feedback scores
- Technical support tickets and resolution times

### Optimization Phase (Months 4-6)
**Quarterly Business Reviews:**
- Comprehensive outcome analysis and reporting
- Expansion planning and additional module discussions
- Reference development and case study creation
- Long-term partnership planning and contract renewal

**Advanced Optimization:**
- Custom scenario development and deployment
- Advanced analytics and reporting implementation
- Research collaboration and publication planning
- Alumni network and continuing education programs

### Renewal & Expansion Phase (Months 7-12)
**Contract Renewal Planning:**
- ROI analysis and business case development
- Competitive analysis and value demonstration
- Expansion planning and additional user licensing
- Multi-year contract negotiation and terms

## Success Metrics & KPIs

### Customer Health Score
**Engagement Metrics (40% weight):**
- Weekly active users (target: 80%+ of licensed users)
- Scenario completion rates (target: 85%+ completion)
- Time spent per session (target: 30-45 minutes)
- Feature adoption rates (target: 90%+ use core features)

**Outcome Metrics (35% weight):**
- Diagnostic accuracy improvement (target: 75%+ improvement)
- User confidence scores (target: 90%+ confidence increase)
- Faculty satisfaction ratings (target: 95%+ satisfaction)
- Student/trainee competency achievement (target: 90%+ pass rate)

**Relationship Metrics (25% weight):**
- Response time to support requests (target: <2 hours)
- Quarterly business review attendance (target: 100%)
- Reference customer willingness (target: 80%+ agree)
- Expansion discussion engagement (target: 90%+ participate)

### Customer Segmentation

#### Tier 1: Strategic Accounts ($25,000+ annual)
**Service Level:**
- Dedicated Customer Success Manager
- Monthly business reviews
- 24/7 priority support
- Custom development and integration
- Executive advisory board participation

#### Tier 2: Growth Accounts ($10,000-25,000 annual)
**Service Level:**
- Shared Customer Success Manager (3-5 accounts)
- Quarterly business reviews
- Business hours priority support
- Standard customization and integration
- Beta program participation

#### Tier 3: Standard Accounts ($5,000-10,000 annual)
**Service Level:**
- Customer Success Specialist support
- Bi-annual business reviews
- Standard business hours support
- Limited customization
- Standard feature access

#### Tier 4: Pilot/SMB Accounts (<$5,000 annual)
**Service Level:**
- Group-based support with specialists
- Annual business reviews
- Standard support channels
- Basic customization
- Standard feature set

## Playbooks & Processes

### Health Score Alert Playbook
**Red Alert (Health Score <60):**
- Immediate CSM notification and escalation
- Executive outreach within 24 hours
- Emergency intervention plan development
- Weekly monitoring and progress tracking

**Yellow Alert (Health Score 60-75):**
- CSM outreach within 48 hours
- Root cause analysis and improvement plan
- Increased support and engagement activities
- Bi-weekly progress reviews

**Green Alert (Health Score >75):**
- Standard quarterly business reviews
- Expansion planning and upsell discussions
- Reference development and case study creation
- Beta program and new feature invitations

### Renewal Playbook
**6 Months Before Renewal:**
- Comprehensive outcome analysis and ROI calculation
- Competitive analysis and value demonstration
- Expansion planning and additional module discussions
- Reference customer agreement and case study development

**3 Months Before Renewal:**
- Contract renewal proposal and pricing discussion
- Multi-year contract options and terms negotiation
- Executive sponsor engagement and relationship building
- Final outcome presentation and business case development

**1 Month Before Renewal:**
- Final contract negotiation and terms confirmation
- Legal review and approval process
- Renewal celebration and partnership announcement
- Next year planning and expansion roadmap

### Expansion Playbook
**Identifying Expansion Opportunities:**
- Usage analytics showing high engagement and success
- Positive feedback and satisfaction scores
- Organic growth in user base within existing accounts
- Requests for additional features or modules

**Expansion Sales Process:**
- CSM identifies expansion opportunity and validates with customer
- Sales team engagement for formal proposal development
- ROI analysis and business case development
- Contract negotiation and implementation planning
