## Research Methodology Framework

### Study Design Standards

#### Randomized Controlled Trials (RCTs)
**Study Design**: Multi-site, double-blind, randomized controlled trials
**Sample Size**: Minimum 100 participants per study arm
**Control Groups**: Active control (traditional training) and waitlist control
**Randomization**: Stratified randomization by experience level and specialty

**Primary Outcomes:**
- **Diagnostic Accuracy**: Standardized patient diagnostic assessment scores
- **Clinical Competency**: Validated clinical competency assessment instruments
- **Skill Retention**: 6, 12, and 24-month skill retention measurements
- **Patient Outcomes**: Patient-reported outcome measures and clinical assessments

#### Mixed-Methods Validation
**Quantitative Components:**
- **Pre/Post Assessments**: Standardized diagnostic accuracy measures
- **Control Group Comparisons**: Traditional training vs. AI-enhanced training
- **Longitudinal Tracking**: 12-36 month follow-up assessments
- **Cost-Effectiveness**: Comprehensive cost-benefit analysis

**Qualitative Components:**
- **Focus Groups**: Practitioner experience and feedback sessions
- **In-Depth Interviews**: Detailed user experience and implementation insights
- **Observational Studies**: Real-world clinical application and outcomes
- **Stakeholder Feedback**: Patient, supervisor, and organizational feedback

### Validation Study Protocols

#### Standardized Assessment Battery
**Primary Assessment Tools:**
- **Standardized Patient Diagnostic Assessment**: Validated diagnostic accuracy measure
- **Clinical Skills Assessment Rubric**: Comprehensive clinical competency evaluation
- **Cultural Competency Assessment**: Validated cultural competency measurement
- **Technology Acceptance Model**: User acceptance and satisfaction measures

**Secondary Assessment Tools:**
- **Patient-Reported Outcome Measures**: Client satisfaction and treatment outcomes
- **Supervisor Evaluations**: Clinical supervisor assessment of practitioner competency
- **Organizational Impact Measures**: System-level impact and implementation outcomes
- **Long-term Follow-up**: 12, 24, and 36-month skill retention and transfer measures
