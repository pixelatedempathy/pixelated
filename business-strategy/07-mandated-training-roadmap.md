# Mandated Training Roadmap - State Certification Integration Strategy

## Executive Summary

This strategic roadmap outlines how to position Pixelated Empathy as a mandated training solution for state-level mental health professional certifications and continuing education units (CEUs). The strategy focuses on becoming an optional but highly recommended path for certification and recertification requirements across multiple states.

**Core Strategy**: Transform from "nice-to-have" to "must-have" by demonstrating superior outcomes, reducing certification costs, improving public safety, and providing measurable competency improvements that exceed traditional training methods.

## 1. State Certification Landscape Analysis

### Current State Requirements

#### Typical CEU Requirements by Profession (Annual):
- **Licensed Psychologists**: 20-40 CEU hours annually
- **Licensed Clinical Social Workers (LCSW)**: 15-30 CEU hours annually
- **Licensed Professional Counselors (LPC)**: 15-25 CEU hours annually
- **Marriage and Family Therapists (MFT)**: 12-20 CEU hours annually
- **Psychiatric Nurses**: 12-24 CEU hours annually

#### Common CEU Categories:
1. **Ethics and Legal Issues** (25-30% of requirements)
2. **Clinical Assessment and Diagnosis** (20-25%)
3. **Cultural Competency** (10-15%)
4. **Crisis Intervention** (10-15%)
5. **Specialized Treatment Approaches** (20-25%)
