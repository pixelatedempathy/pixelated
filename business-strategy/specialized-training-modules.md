# Phase 2 Specialized Training Modules

Advanced training modules for Phase 2 market expansion (Months 13-24), targeting specialized mental health populations and advanced therapeutic approaches to expand market reach and deepen clinical impact.

**Months 13-18**: Core specialized modules launch

## Specialized Training Modules

### Module 1: Trauma-Informed Care Excellence

#### Target Audience
- **Primary**: Trauma specialists, PTSD treatment providers, crisis counselors
- **Secondary**: General mental health practitioners working with trauma survivors
- **Tertiary**: Emergency responders, social workers, medical professionals

#### Training Content Structure
**Foundation Level (Weeks 1-4)**
- **Trauma Theory**: Comprehensive trauma theory and neurobiology
- **Trauma Types**: Acute, chronic, complex trauma presentations
- **Assessment Tools**: PTSD, CPTSD, and trauma screening instruments
- **Safety Planning**: Trauma-informed safety assessment and planning

**Intermediate Level (Weeks 5-8)**
- **Evidence-Based Interventions**: EMDR, TF-CBT, Somatic approaches
- **Case Formulation**: Trauma-informed case conceptualization
- **Treatment Planning**: Individualized trauma treatment planning
- **Crisis Management**: Acute trauma response and crisis intervention

**Advanced Level (Weeks 9-12)**
- **Complex Trauma**: Dissociation, personality disorders, complex presentations
- **Trauma Systems**: Working with trauma systems and organizations
- **Vicarious Trauma**: Practitioner self-care and trauma exposure management
- **Specialized Populations**: Veterans, refugees, domestic violence survivors

#### Advanced Scenarios (50+ Scenarios)
**Complex PTSD Cases:**
- **Military Veterans**: Combat-related PTSD with complex comorbidity
- **Refugee Populations**: War trauma and acculturation stress
- **Domestic Violence**: Complex trauma with ongoing safety concerns
- **Childhood Abuse**: Developmental trauma and attachment disorders

**Crisis Intervention Scenarios:**
- **Acute Trauma Response**: Recent trauma exposure and immediate intervention
- **Suicidal Ideation**: Trauma-related suicidal risk assessment and management
- **Dissociative Episodes**: Complex trauma with dissociative presentations
- **Re-traumatization**: Preventing and managing re-traumatization in therapy

#### Clinical Validation
**Outcome Measures:**
- **PTSD Checklist (PCL-5)**: Pre/post trauma symptom assessment
- **Complex PTSD Scale**: Specialized complex trauma assessment
- **Trauma-Informed Practice Scale**: Practitioner competency measurement
- **Client Satisfaction**: Trauma-informed care satisfaction scores

**Target Improvement**: 80%+ improvement in trauma-informed care competency

### Module 2: Addiction & Substance Use Treatment

#### Target Audience
- **Primary**: Addiction counselors, substance use specialists, dual diagnosis providers
- **Secondary**: Mental health practitioners working with co-occurring disorders
- **Tertiary**: Primary care physicians, emergency department staff

#### Training Content Structure
**Assessment & Screening (Weeks 1-3)**
- **Substance Use Disorders**: DSM-5 criteria and assessment approaches
- **Screening Tools**: AUDIT, DAST, CAGE, and specialized screening instruments
- **Dual Diagnosis**: Co-occurring mental health and substance use disorders
- **Motivational Interviewing**: MI techniques for substance use treatment

**Treatment Approaches (Weeks 4-8)**
- **Evidence-Based Interventions**: CBT, DBT, 12-step facilitation, harm reduction
- **Medication-Assisted Treatment**: MAT integration and monitoring
- **Relapse Prevention**: Comprehensive relapse prevention planning
- **Family Systems**: Addiction family dynamics and intervention

**Specialized Populations (Weeks 9-12)**
- **Adolescents**: Youth substance use and developmentally appropriate interventions
- **Older Adults**: Substance use in aging populations
- **Chronic Pain**: Pain management and substance use disorders
- **Criminal Justice**: Substance use treatment in forensic settings

#### Advanced Scenarios (45+ Scenarios)
**Complex Addiction Cases:**
- **Opioid Use Disorder**: MAT integration and complex medical comorbidity
- **Alcohol Use Disorder**: Severe alcohol dependence and withdrawal management
- **Polysubstance Use**: Multiple substance use and complex treatment planning
- **Dual Diagnosis**: Schizophrenia and substance use, depression and addiction

**Crisis Scenarios:**
- **Overdose Response**: Opioid overdose and emergency intervention
- **Withdrawal Management**: Acute withdrawal and medical management
- **Suicidal Ideation**: Substance use and suicidal risk assessment
- **Family Crisis**: Addiction-related family crisis intervention

#### Clinical Validation
**Outcome Measures:**
- **AUDIT/DAST**: Substance use screening and monitoring
- **Addiction Severity Index**: Comprehensive addiction assessment
- **Readiness to Change Scale**: Motivation and treatment readiness
- **Quality of Life Measures**: Addiction-related quality of life improvement

**Target Improvement**: 75%+ improvement in addiction treatment competency

### Module 4: Advanced Therapeutic Modalities

#### Target Audience
- **Primary**: Experienced mental health practitioners seeking advanced training
- **Secondary**: Specialist practitioners in specific therapeutic modalities
- **Tertiary**: Clinical supervisors and training program faculty

#### Training Content Structure
**EMDR Training (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing)
- **EMDR Foundation**: Theoretical foundations and 8-phase protocol
- **Complex Cases**: Complex trauma and dissociation treatment
- **Specialized Applications**: Phobias, anxiety, performance enhancement
- **Supervision & Consultation**: EMDR case consultation and supervision

**DBT Skills Training (Dialectical Behavior Therapy)
- **DBT Fundamentals**: Biosocial theory and treatment assumptions
- **Skills Training Modules**: Mindfulness, distress tolerance, emotion regulation, interpersonal effectiveness
- **Individual Therapy**: DBT individual therapy and case management
- **Consultation Team**: DBT consultation team and program development

**ACT Training (Acceptance and Commitment Therapy)
- **ACT Foundation**: Psychological flexibility model and hexaflex
- **Clinical Applications**: Anxiety, depression, chronic pain, trauma
- **Values Work**: Values clarification and committed action planning
- **Behavioral Activation**: ACT-informed behavioral activation approaches

#### Advanced Scenarios (40+ Scenarios)
**EMDR Complex Cases:**
- **Complex PTSD**: Multiple trauma presentations and dissociation
- **Phobia Treatment**: Specific phobias and performance anxiety
- **Grief & Loss**: Complicated grief and trauma processing
- **Performance Enhancement**: Sports and professional performance issues

**DBT Complex Cases:**
- **Borderline Personality Disorder**: Complex BPD presentations and treatment
- **Self-Harm Behaviors**: Non-suicidal self-injury and suicidal behaviors
- **Emotional Dysregulation**: Severe emotion regulation difficulties
- **Interpersonal Problems**: Complex relationship and attachment issues

#### Clinical Validation
**Outcome Measures:**
- **EMDR Fidelity Scale**: Treatment fidelity and competency assessment
- **DBT Skills Assessment**: DBT skills acquisition and application
- **ACT Process Measures**: Psychological flexibility and ACT processes
- **Clinical Outcomes**: Symptom improvement and functional outcomes

**Target Improvement**: 85%+ improvement in specialized modality competency

## Certification & Accreditation Programs

### Professional Certification Tracks

#### Trauma-Informed Care Certification
**Level 1: Foundation Certification**
- **Requirements**: Complete trauma-informed care module + 20 hours supervised practice
- **Certification**: "Certified Trauma-Informed Practitioner" designation
- **Continuing Education**: 20 hours annual continuing education
- **Renewal**: Annual renewal with competency demonstration

**Level 2: Advanced Certification**
- **Requirements**: Level 1 + advanced module + 50 hours supervised practice
- **Certification**: "Certified Advanced Trauma Practitioner" designation
- **Supervision**: Peer supervision and consultation requirements
- **Renewal**: Biennial renewal with advanced competency demonstration

#### Specialized Population Certifications
**Child & Adolescent Mental Health Certification**
- **Requirements**: Complete child/adolescent module + 30 hours supervised practice
- **Certification**: "Certified Child & Adolescent Mental Health Practitioner"
- **Practicum**: Supervised clinical practicum with child/adolescent populations
- **Renewal**: Annual renewal with continuing education requirements

**Cultural Competency Certification**
- **Requirements**: Complete cultural competency module + diversity training
- **Certification**: "Culturally Competent Mental Health Practitioner"
- **Cultural Immersion**: Cultural immersion experience or community engagement
- **Renewal**: Annual renewal with cultural competency updates
