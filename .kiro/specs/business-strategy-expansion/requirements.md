# Requirements Document: Business Strategy Expansion & CMS System

## Introduction

This specification outlines the comprehensive expansion of Pixelated Empathy's business strategy documentation and the development of a collaborative CMS/backend system to support the marketing and business development team. The project addresses four critical areas: niche market opportunities, competitive differentiation, grassroots/free marketing strategies, and technical infrastructure for team collaboration.

## Glossary

- **System**: The Pixelated Empathy business strategy expansion and CMS platform
- **CMS**: Content Management System for business strategy documentation
- **Niche_Market**: Underserved or specialized market segments not currently targeted
- **Grassroots_Marketing**: Community-driven, organic, zero-cost promotional activities
- **Competitive_Moat**: Unique advantages that prevent competitors from replicating our value
- **User**: Team member accessing the CMS system (marketing, sales, business development)
- **Content_Creator**: Team member who creates or edits business strategy content
- **Viewer**: Team member with read-only access to business strategy content
- **Administrator**: Team member with full system access and user management capabilities

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Niche Market Opportunity Identification

**User Story:** As a business strategist, I want to identify and document underserved niche markets, so that we can capture opportunities competitors are overlooking.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL identify at least 10 distinct niche market opportunities not currently addressed in existing strategy documents
2. WHEN analyzing niche markets, THE System SHALL document market size, accessibility, and competitive landscape for each opportunity
3. THE System SHALL prioritize niche markets based on alignment with core competencies, resource requirements, and potential ROI
4. THE System SHALL include specific go-to-market strategies for each identified niche market
5. THE System SHALL document barriers to entry and competitive advantages for each niche opportunity

### Requirement 2: Rural and Underserved Population Strategy

**User Story:** As a market expansion lead, I want detailed strategies for reaching rural and underserved populations, so that we can expand our impact and market reach.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL document specific challenges faced by rural mental health professionals including access, training costs, and geographic isolation
2. WHEN developing rural strategies, THE System SHALL include pricing models adapted for lower-income markets
3. THE System SHALL identify partnership opportunities with rural healthcare organizations, community health centers, and telehealth providers
4. THE System SHALL document technology requirements and infrastructure considerations for rural deployment
5. THE System SHALL include success metrics specific to rural market penetration and impact

### Requirement 3: Demographic-Specific Market Strategies

**User Story:** As a product marketing manager, I want strategies targeting specific demographics (age, specialty, practice type), so that we can create highly targeted campaigns.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL document strategies for at least 5 distinct demographic segments including early-career therapists, mid-career specialists, late-career practitioners, academic researchers, and corporate wellness professionals
2. WHEN defining demographic strategies, THE System SHALL include persona profiles, pain points, value propositions, and messaging frameworks
3. THE System SHALL document channel preferences and communication strategies for each demographic segment
4. THE System SHALL include pricing and packaging strategies optimized for each demographic
5. THE System SHALL provide content templates and campaign frameworks for each demographic segment

### Requirement 4: Competitive Intelligence and Differentiation

**User Story:** As a competitive analyst, I want comprehensive analysis of what competitors are doing that we aren't, so that we can identify gaps and opportunities.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL document at least 15 direct and indirect competitors with detailed analysis of their strengths, weaknesses, and market positioning
2. WHEN analyzing competitors, THE System SHALL identify specific features, services, or strategies they offer that we currently don't
3. THE System SHALL provide recommendations for each competitive gap including "adopt and improve", "ignore", or "differentiate differently"
4. THE System SHALL document our unique competitive advantages that competitors cannot easily replicate
5. THE System SHALL include a competitive response playbook for common competitive scenarios

### Requirement 5: Competitive Feature Gap Analysis

**User Story:** As a product manager, I want to know what features competitors have that we lack, so that we can prioritize development roadmap.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL create a feature comparison matrix across all major competitors
2. WHEN identifying feature gaps, THE System SHALL categorize them as "critical", "important", or "nice-to-have"
3. THE System SHALL provide implementation recommendations including effort, cost, and expected impact for each gap
4. THE System SHALL document customer feedback related to competitive feature gaps
5. THE System SHALL track feature gap closure over time with quarterly reviews

### Requirement 6: Competitive Pricing and Business Model Analysis

**User Story:** As a pricing strategist, I want detailed analysis of competitor pricing and business models, so that we can optimize our own pricing strategy.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL document pricing models for all major competitors including tiers, features, and value propositions
2. WHEN analyzing pricing, THE System SHALL calculate our price positioning relative to competitors (premium, mid-market, value)
3. THE System SHALL identify pricing strategies competitors use that we could adopt or improve upon
4. THE System SHALL document customer perception of value relative to price for our platform and competitors
5. THE System SHALL provide pricing optimization recommendations based on competitive analysis

### Requirement 7: Grassroots Marketing Strategy Framework

**User Story:** As a marketing manager with limited budget, I want comprehensive grassroots marketing strategies, so that we can grow organically without paid advertising.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL document at least 20 distinct grassroots marketing tactics with implementation guides
2. WHEN defining grassroots tactics, THE System SHALL include required resources, expected timeline, and success metrics
3. THE System SHALL prioritize tactics based on effort-to-impact ratio and alignment with brand values
4. THE System SHALL include templates, scripts, and resources for executing each tactic
5. THE System SHALL document case studies and success stories from similar organizations

### Requirement 8: Community Building and Engagement Strategy

**User Story:** As a community manager, I want strategies for building authentic communities around our platform, so that we can create organic advocates and word-of-mouth growth.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL document community building strategies across at least 5 platforms (LinkedIn, Reddit, professional forums, Slack/Discord, email communities)
2. WHEN defining community strategies, THE System SHALL include content calendars, engagement tactics, and moderation guidelines
3. THE System SHALL provide frameworks for identifying and nurturing community champions and advocates
4. THE System SHALL include metrics for measuring community health and engagement
5. THE System SHALL document crisis management and conflict resolution procedures for community management

### Requirement 9: Content Marketing and Thought Leadership

**User Story:** As a content marketer, I want detailed content strategies that establish thought leadership without paid promotion, so that we can build authority and trust organically.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL document content strategies for at least 10 channels including blog, podcast, YouTube, LinkedIn, Medium, academic publications, conference presentations, webinars, social media, and email newsletters
2. WHEN defining content strategies, THE System SHALL include topic frameworks, content calendars, and distribution plans
3. THE System SHALL provide templates and guidelines for each content type
4. THE System SHALL document SEO strategies and keyword targeting for organic discovery
5. THE System SHALL include metrics for measuring content effectiveness and ROI

### Requirement 10: Partnership and Collaboration Strategies

**User Story:** As a partnership manager, I want strategies for building mutually beneficial partnerships that provide free marketing exposure, so that we can leverage other organizations' audiences.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL identify at least 50 potential partnership opportunities across academic institutions, professional associations, healthcare organizations, technology companies, and media outlets
2. WHEN defining partnerships, THE System SHALL include value propositions for partners, collaboration models, and success metrics
3. THE System SHALL provide outreach templates and negotiation frameworks for partnership development
4. THE System SHALL document case studies of successful partnerships in similar industries
5. THE System SHALL include partnership tracking and management workflows

### Requirement 11: User-Generated Content and Advocacy Programs

**User Story:** As a growth marketer, I want programs that turn customers into advocates who create content and promote our platform, so that we can scale marketing through our user base.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL document at least 5 distinct advocacy programs including referral programs, case study programs, testimonial programs, ambassador programs, and user-generated content campaigns
2. WHEN defining advocacy programs, THE System SHALL include incentive structures, participation requirements, and success metrics
3. THE System SHALL provide templates and guidelines for collecting and showcasing user-generated content
4. THE System SHALL document legal and compliance considerations for user-generated content
5. THE System SHALL include tracking mechanisms for measuring advocacy program effectiveness

### Requirement 12: CMS System Architecture and User Management

**User Story:** As a system administrator, I want a robust CMS system with role-based access control, so that team members can collaborate effectively while maintaining security.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL support at least 4 user roles: Administrator, Content_Creator, Editor, and Viewer
2. WHEN managing users, THE System SHALL provide role-based permissions for create, read, update, and delete operations
3. THE System SHALL include user authentication and session management with secure password requirements
4. THE System SHALL log all user actions for audit and compliance purposes
5. THE System SHALL support user invitation workflows and onboarding processes

### Requirement 13: Content Creation and Editing Interface

**User Story:** As a content creator with limited technical skills, I want an intuitive content editing interface, so that I can create and update business strategy documents without technical assistance.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL provide a WYSIWYG (What You See Is What You Get) editor for content creation
2. WHEN editing content, THE System SHALL support rich text formatting, images, tables, and embedded media
3. THE System SHALL include content templates for common document types (market analysis, competitive analysis, marketing plans)
4. THE System SHALL provide real-time collaboration features allowing multiple users to edit simultaneously
5. THE System SHALL include auto-save functionality to prevent content loss

### Requirement 14: Version Control and Content History

**User Story:** As a content manager, I want comprehensive version control for all documents, so that I can track changes, revert to previous versions, and understand document evolution.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL maintain complete version history for all documents with timestamps and user attribution
2. WHEN viewing version history, THE System SHALL provide diff views showing changes between versions
3. THE System SHALL allow users to revert to any previous version with a single action
4. THE System SHALL support version comparison across multiple versions simultaneously
5. THE System SHALL include commenting and annotation features for version review

### Requirement 15: Content Organization and Navigation

**User Story:** As a team member, I want intuitive content organization and navigation, so that I can quickly find relevant business strategy information.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL organize content hierarchically with categories, subcategories, and tags
2. WHEN searching for content, THE System SHALL provide full-text search with filters and advanced search options
3. THE System SHALL include a visual sitemap or content tree for navigation
4. THE System SHALL support custom views and saved searches for frequently accessed content
5. THE System SHALL provide breadcrumb navigation and related content suggestions

### Requirement 16: Workflow and Approval Processes

**User Story:** As a content approver, I want structured workflow and approval processes, so that content quality is maintained before publication.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL support customizable approval workflows with multiple approval stages
2. WHEN content requires approval, THE System SHALL notify designated approvers and track approval status
3. THE System SHALL allow approvers to provide feedback and request revisions
4. THE System SHALL prevent publication of content until all required approvals are obtained
5. THE System SHALL maintain audit trails of all approval decisions and feedback

### Requirement 17: Export and Integration Capabilities

**User Story:** As a business analyst, I want to export content in multiple formats and integrate with other tools, so that I can use business strategy data across different platforms.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL support export to at least 5 formats: PDF, Word, Markdown, HTML, and JSON
2. WHEN exporting content, THE System SHALL preserve formatting, images, and document structure
3. THE System SHALL provide API endpoints for programmatic access to content
4. THE System SHALL integrate with common productivity tools (Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Slack)
5. THE System SHALL support bulk export and import operations for content migration

### Requirement 18: Analytics and Reporting

**User Story:** As a marketing director, I want analytics on content usage and team collaboration, so that I can measure effectiveness and optimize our business strategy processes.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL track content views, edits, and user engagement metrics
2. WHEN generating reports, THE System SHALL provide insights on most-viewed content, active contributors, and collaboration patterns
3. THE System SHALL include dashboards for real-time monitoring of system usage
4. THE System SHALL support custom report creation with filters and date ranges
5. THE System SHALL provide export capabilities for all analytics data

### Requirement 19: Mobile Accessibility and Responsive Design

**User Story:** As a team member who travels frequently, I want mobile access to business strategy content, so that I can reference information on the go.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL provide responsive design that adapts to mobile, tablet, and desktop screens
2. WHEN accessing on mobile, THE System SHALL maintain full functionality including viewing, editing, and collaboration
3. THE System SHALL optimize content loading for mobile networks with reduced bandwidth
4. THE System SHALL support offline access to recently viewed content
5. THE System SHALL provide mobile-optimized navigation and search interfaces

### Requirement 20: Security and Compliance

**User Story:** As a security officer, I want robust security measures and compliance features, so that sensitive business strategy information is protected.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL encrypt all data in transit using TLS 1.3 and at rest using AES-256
2. WHEN handling sensitive information, THE System SHALL support data classification and access restrictions
3. THE System SHALL include security audit logging for all access and modifications
4. THE System SHALL comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other relevant data protection regulations
5. THE System SHALL provide regular security updates and vulnerability patching
