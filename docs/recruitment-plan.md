# Recruitment Plan – Phase 1.3 Annotation & Labeling

**Objective**: Build a high‑quality labeled dataset for the Pixel
therapeutic‑enhancement model with **Cohen’s Kappa > 0.85** and full coverage
of all 5 000+ samples.

---

## 1. Target Profile

### Role Categories

- **Clinical Annotator**:
  - Credentials: MA/PhD in Psychology, Psychiatry, Social Work.
  - Exp: 1-2 years clinical documentation or case review.
  - Duties: Apply taxonomy, flag triggers, provide notes.
- **QA Annotator**:
  - Credentials: Research reliability experience.
  - Exp: Cohen’s Kappa calculations.
  - Duties: Double-blind reviews, calculate Kappa, resolve conflicts.
- **Safety Screening Specialist**:
  - Credentials: Licensed clinician with risk-assessment training.
  - Exp: Crisis hotline or emergency response.
  - Duties: Screen safety triggers, ensure escalation.

---

## 2. Sourcing Channels

### Sourcing Channels

- **Professional Networks**: Post "Research Study - Paid Opportunity" on
  LinkedIn.
- **Academic Depts**: Contact secretaries for graduate student worker pools.
- **Practice Networks**: Reach out to practice managers via newsletters.
- **Freelance Platforms**: Use vetted platforms with screening quizzes.
- **Referral Program**: $50 bonus per successful hire.

---

## 3. Screening & Selection Process

1. **Application Form** – Collect credentials, licensure, CV, availability, and
   statement of interest.
2. **Screening Quiz** – 5‑question multiple‑choice test covering:
   - Label definitions (e.g., difference between `Suicidal` and `Self‑Harm`)
   - Safety‑trigger identification
   - Basic inter‑rater reliability concepts
3. **Live Calibration Exercise** – Provide 3 samples; ask to label and justify.
   Senior annotator reviews for conceptual accuracy.
4. **Background Check** - Verify licensure status via state licensing board
   (if applicable).
5. **Offer & Onboarding** - Send compensation details, confidentiality
   agreement, and platform access instructions.

_Target throughput:_ 15 qualified annotators within the first 2 weeks to allow
parallel double-blind labeling.

---

## 4. Training & Calibration

### Training Modules

- **Platform Walk-through** (30 min): Tooling and submission flow.
- **Label Taxonomy** (45 min): Deep-dive into categories and edge cases.
- **Safety Protocol** (20 min): Escalation chain and documentation.
- **Quality Control** (30 min): Double-blind workflow and Kappa concepts.
- **Mock Session** (60 min): Annotate 5 items with immediate feedback.

All training materials (slides, recordings, FAQ) are stored in the `training/`
folder of the repository for version control.

## 6. Timeline

### Timeline

- **Wk 1**: Post ads, collect apps, begin screening quiz.
- **Wk 2**: Complete screening, calibration, and background checks.
- **Wk 3**: Training modules, mock sessions, finalize roster.
- **Wk 4**: Double-blind pilot set (200 items) + provisional Kappa.
- **Wk 5**: Refine guidelines; scale to full dataset.

## 7. Success Metrics (Recruitment Phase)

- **Qualified Applicant Yield:** >= 30 applicants from combined channels.
- **Screening Pass Rate:** >= 60% pass the screening quiz.
- **Training Completion:** 100% of hired annotators finish all modules.
- **Initial Kappa (pilot set):** >= 0.80 before full-scale labeling begins.
- **Time-to-Hire:** <= 14 days from ad posting to offer acceptance.
