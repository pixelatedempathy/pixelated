# Founder Story - Google Cloud Startup Application

## The Journey: From Addiction Counselor to AI Developer

### The Problem That Changed Everything

In 2022, I was working as an addiction counselor and attachment specialist at a busy mental health clinic. Every day, I watched new therapists struggle with the same challenges:

- **Inconsistent training quality** across different supervisors and institutions
- **Real-time feedback gaps** - therapists only learned about mistakes months later during supervision
- **Unconscious bias** affecting therapeutic relationships, often going unrecognized
- **Training costs** averaging $15,000 per therapist annually while delivering variable results
- **Burnout rates** climbing as therapists felt unprepared for complex cases

The breaking point came when I realized these weren't isolated incidents—they were systemic failures affecting thousands of mental health professionals nationwide.

### The Pivot: When Domain Expertise Met Technology

As a mental health professional, I understood exactly what therapists needed. As someone who had always been fascinated by technology, I saw how AI could solve these problems. The challenge? I needed to become the bridge between two worlds that rarely intersect.

**My Self-Taught Journey (18 months):**
- **Month 1-3**: Python for data analysis and AI fundamentals
- **Month 4-6**: Full-stack web development (React, Node.js, databases)
- **Month 7-9**: Cloud architecture and DevOps (AWS, Docker, CI/CD)
- **Month 10-12**: AI/ML specialization (TensorFlow, NLP, bias detection)
- **Month 13-15**: Security and compliance (HIPAA, encryption, audit systems)
- **Month 16-18**: Enterprise architecture and scalability

- **Security**: HIPAA compliance with FHE encryption and comprehensive audit logging

**Enterprise Features:**
