# Data Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: January 2025  
**Effective Date**: January 2025

## Introduction

Pixelated Empathy ("we," "our," "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our therapeutic AI platform.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information**: Name, email address, username, password
- **Profile Information**: Demographics, preferences, settings
- **Session Data**: Conversation history, therapeutic interactions, timestamps
- **Device Information**: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers

### Health Information (Protected Health Information - PHI)
- **Mental Health Content**: Thoughts, feelings, emotions, therapeutic conversations
- **Clinical Data**: Session notes, progress tracking, treatment history
- **Crisis Information**: Safety assessments, risk evaluations, intervention records
- **Referral Data**: Professional referrals, treatment recommendations

### Technical Information
- **Usage Analytics**: Features used, interaction patterns, system performance
- **Log Data**: Error logs, system logs, access logs
- **Cookies and Tracking**: Session cookies, authentication tokens, analytics cookies

## How We Use Your Information

### Service Delivery
- Provide therapeutic AI services and support
- Process and respond to your conversations
- Deliver personalized therapeutic interactions
- Maintain session continuity and history

### Safety and Crisis Management
- Detect and respond to crisis situations
- Assess safety risks
- Escalate to professional help when necessary
- Maintain safety audit trails

### Service Improvement
- Analyze usage patterns to improve services
- Develop and enhance therapeutic capabilities
- Train and improve AI models (with proper anonymization)
- Conduct research (with consent and IRB approval)

### Legal and Compliance
- Comply with HIPAA, GDPR, and other regulations
- Respond to legal requests and court orders
- Protect rights and safety of users and others
- Enforce terms of service

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

### We Do NOT Sell Your Data
Pixelated Empathy does not sell, rent, or trade personal information or PHI.

### Permitted Sharing
We may share information in the following circumstances:

1. **Professional Referrals**: With your explicit consent, we may share relevant information with licensed mental health professionals for treatment purposes.

2. **Safety Emergencies**: We may disclose information to emergency services or crisis intervention services when we have a good faith belief that there is a risk of harm.

3. **Business Associates**: We may share information with service providers who are bound by Business Associate Agreements (BAA) and strict confidentiality requirements.

4. **Legal Requirements**: When required by law, court order, or government request.

5. **Business Transfers**: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notice to users).

### De-identified Information
We may use and share de-identified, aggregated data for research and analytics that cannot be linked back to individual users.

## Data Security

### Security Measures
- **Encryption**: End-to-end encryption for data in transit (TLS 1.3)
- **Storage Encryption**: AES-256 encryption for data at rest
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access control and multi-factor authentication
- **Audit Logging**: Comprehensive audit trails for all data access
- **Network Security**: Firewalls, intrusion detection, DDoS protection
- **Regular Security Audits**: Third-party security assessments

### Breach Notification
In the event of a data breach affecting your PHI, we will:
- Notify you within 72 hours of discovery
- Report to relevant authorities as required by law
- Provide guidance on protective measures
- Conduct a post-incident review

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Portability
- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal information and PHI
- **Portability**: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- **Export**: Download your session history and data

### Correction and Updates
- **Correct**: Request correction of inaccurate information
- **Update**: Modify your account and profile information
- **Supplement**: Add additional information to your records

### Deletion and Retention
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and data
- **Retention**: Understand our data retention policies
- **Exceptions**: Be aware of legal retention requirements

### Consent Management
- **Withdraw Consent**: Revoke consent for data processing
- **Opt-Out**: Opt out of non-essential data processing
- **Marketing**: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

### Restrictions and Objections
- **Processing Restrictions**: Request limitations on how we process your data
- **Objections**: Object to certain types of data processing

## Data Retention

### Retention Periods
- **Active Accounts**: Data retained while account is active
- **Inactive Accounts**: Data retained for 7 years after last activity
- **Clinical Records**: Minimum 6 years retention (HIPAA requirement)
- **Audit Logs**: 6 years retention for compliance
- **Crisis Records**: 7 years retention for safety purposes

### Deletion
Upon account deletion request:
- Data will be deleted within 30 days
- Legal exceptions may apply (investigations, litigation)
- De-identified data may be retained for research

## International Data Transfers

### Cross-Border Transfers
- Data may be processed in the United States and other countries
- Transfers comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable laws
- Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) used where required
- Adequate safeguards maintained for international transfers

## Children's Privacy

Pixelated Empathy is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we discover we have collected information from a child, we will delete it immediately.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will:
- Notify you of material changes via email or platform notification
- Post the updated policy with a new "Last Updated" date
- Obtain consent for significant changes where required by law

## Regional Addendums

### California Residents (CCPA)
California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including:
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of sale of personal information
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising rights

### European Union (GDPR)
EU residents have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including those listed in the "Your Rights and Choices" section above. Our Data Protection Officer can assist with GDPR-related inquiries.

### Health Information (HIPAA)
Protected Health Information (PHI) is subject to additional protections under HIPAA. Our Notice of Privacy Practices provides detailed information about HIPAA rights and protections.
