# Cultural Adaptation Framework for International Markets

## Overview
A comprehensive framework for adapting Pixelated Empathy's AI-powered mental health training platform for international markets, ensuring cultural competency, regulatory compliance, and market-specific relevance while maintaining clinical excellence.

## Adaptation Principles

### Core Framework Pillars

#### 1. Cultural Competency Integration
**Definition**: Systematic integration of cultural factors, beliefs, and practices into mental health training scenarios
**Objective**: Ensure training effectiveness across diverse cultural contexts while maintaining diagnostic accuracy

#### 2. Regulatory Compliance Alignment
**Definition**: Adaptation to meet specific regulatory, legal, and professional standards in target markets
**Objective**: Ensure full compliance with local healthcare and professional requirements

#### 3. Linguistic & Communication Adaptation
**Definition**: Modification of language, terminology, and communication styles for target markets
**Objective**: Ensure clear understanding and cultural appropriateness of training content

#### 4. Healthcare System Integration
**Definition**: Alignment with local healthcare systems, referral pathways, and treatment approaches
**Objective**: Ensure practical applicability and system integration in target markets

**Clinical Language:**
- **ICD-11 Preference**: UK preference over DSM-5 diagnostic criteria
- **NICE Guidelines**: Integration with National Institute for Health and Care Excellence standards
- **\"Presenting Complaint\"**: UK healthcare documentation standard
- **\"Stepped Care Model\"**: NHS mental health service delivery approach
