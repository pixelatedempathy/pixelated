# Pixelated Empathy - Comprehensive Pricing Strategy

## Overview
A strategic pricing framework designed to maximize revenue across different market segments while ensuring accessibility and value delivery across academic, healthcare, and individual practitioner markets.

## Pricing Philosophy

### Value-Based Pricing Framework
**Core Principle**: Price based on measurable value delivery and ROI to customers
**Value Metrics**: Diagnostic accuracy improvement, time savings, risk reduction, patient outcomes
**ROI Targets**: 300-500% ROI for institutional customers within 12 months

### Market Segmentation Strategy
**Tiered Approach**: Different pricing tiers based on customer type, usage, and feature requirements
**Geographic Variation**: Market-specific pricing for domestic vs. international markets
**Competitive Positioning**: Premium pricing justified by superior clinical outcomes and AI capabilities
