# Pixelated Empathy - Professional Association Partnership Strategy

## Overview
A comprehensive strategy for establishing strategic partnerships with leading professional associations in mental health, positioning Pixelated Empathy as the preferred training solution for continuing education and professional development.

## Target Professional Associations

### Tier 1: Primary Mental Health Associations

#### American Psychological Association (APA)
**Strategic Priority**: Highest
**Opportunity**: 157,000+ members, premier psychology organization
**Partnership Approach**: Division 17 (Society of Counseling Psychology) and Division 12 (Clinical Psychology)

**Key Initiatives:**
- **Committee Participation**: Continuing Education Committee
- **Conference Presence**: Annual Convention (15,000+ attendees)
- **Publication Opportunities**: Monitor on Psychology, American Psychologist
- **Special Interest Groups**: Technology and Innovation in Psychology

**Engagement Timeline:**
- **Q1 2025**: Initial contact and relationship building
- **Q2 2025**: Pilot program proposal and agreement
- **Q3 2025**: First joint webinar and content collaboration
- **Q4 2025**: Annual convention presence and member benefits launch

#### American Counseling Association (ACA)
**Strategic Priority**: Highest
**Opportunity**: 60,000+ members, counseling profession focus
**Partnership Approach**: Technology Interest Network and Continuing Education Committee

**Key Initiatives:**

- **Conference Integration**: ACA Annual Conference & Expo
- **Member Benefits**: Exclusive discounts and early access

#### National Association of Social Workers (NASW)
**Strategic Priority**: High
