When writing backup files, always: 1) Create parent directories with mkdir -p or mkdirSync({recursive: true}), 2) Add timestamps to filenames to prevent overwriting previous backups, 3) Use format like YYYYMMDD_HHMMSS or ISO format with special chars replaced
