# Product Overview

Pixelated Empathy is an AI-powered mental health training platform that provides empathy-driven technology for mental health professionals and therapeutic applications.

## Core Mission

Build empathy-driven technology that prioritizes human connection, psychological safety, and ethical AI. The platform focuses on understanding not just what people say, but why they said it, how they felt, and what they really needed.
