## Compensation Framework

### Base Salary Structure

#### Base Salary Ranges by Level
**Entry Level (SDR/ISR)**: $45,000-$70,000
**Mid-Level (AE)**: $70,000-$120,000
**Senior Level (Senior AE)**: $90,000-$150,000
**Executive Level (Enterprise AE)**: $120,000-$180,000
**Leadership Level (VP)**: $150,000-$250,000

### Commission Structure

#### Commission Plan Overview
**Commission Rate**: 8-12% of annual recurring revenue
**Accelerators**: 1.5x for 100-125% quota, 2x for 125-150% quota, 2.5x for 150%+ quota
**Decelerators**: 0.5x for 50-75% quota, 0% below 50% quota
**Clawback**: 100% clawback for cancellations within 12 months

#### Commission Tiers
**Tier 1 - Entry**: 8% commission rate, $250K-$400K quota
**Tier 2 - Mid**: 10% commission rate, $400K-$750K quota
**Tier 3 - Senior**: 12% commission rate, $750K-$1.5M quota
**Tier 4 - Executive**: 12% commission rate, $1.5M-$3M quota

### Bonus Structure

#### Quarterly Bonuses
**Quota Achievement Bonus:**
- 100-110% quota: $5,000 bonus
- 110-125% quota: $10,000 bonus
- 125-150% quota: $15,000 bonus
- 150%+ quota: $25,000 bonus

**Special Achievement Bonuses:**
- **New Logo Bonus**: $2,500-$10,000 for new enterprise accounts
- **Deal Size Bonus**: $5,000-$25,000 for deals over $100K
- **Vertical Market Bonus**: $2,500 for deals in target verticals
- **Team Bonus**: $5,000-$15,000 for team quota achievement

#### Annual Bonuses
**President's Club**: Top 10% performers
- **Trip**: All-expenses-paid luxury trip
- **Bonus**: $10,000-$25,000 additional bonus
- **Recognition**: Company-wide recognition and awards

### Equity Compensation

#### Equity Allocation by Role
**CRO**: 0.5% equity over 4 years
**VP Sales**: 0.3% equity over 4 years
**Enterprise AE**: 0.1% equity over 4 years
**Senior AE**: 0.05% equity over 4 years
**AE**: 0.02% equity over 4 years

#### Vesting Schedule
- **Year 1**: 25% vesting after 12 months
- **Years 2-4**: Monthly vesting over remaining 36 months
- **Acceleration**: Double-trigger acceleration on change of control

## Sales Process & Methodology

### Sales Process Framework

#### Enterprise Sales Process (6-18 months)
**Phase 1: Discovery (Weeks 1-4)**
- **Activities**: Needs assessment, stakeholder mapping, competitive analysis
- **Tools**: Discovery questionnaire, stakeholder analysis matrix
- **Success Metrics**: 3+ stakeholder meetings, qualified opportunity criteria met

**Phase 2: Solution Design (Weeks 5-8)**
- **Activities**: Custom solution development, ROI analysis, technical requirements
- **Tools**: Solution design workshop, ROI calculator, technical specification
- **Success Metrics**: Solution approval, budget confirmation, technical fit

**Phase 3: Proposal & Negotiation (Weeks 9-12)**
- **Activities**: Formal proposal, legal review, contract negotiation
- **Tools**: Proposal template, legal review checklist, negotiation framework
- **Success Metrics**: Contract terms agreed, signature authority confirmed

**Phase 4: Closing & Onboarding (Weeks 13-16)**
- **Activities**: Contract execution, project kickoff, customer success handoff
- **Tools**: Contract execution checklist, onboarding plan, success metrics
- **Success Metrics**: Contract signed, onboarding initiated, success metrics defined

#### Mid-Market Sales Process (3-6 months)
**Phase 1: Qualification (Weeks 1-2)**
- **Activities**: BANT qualification, needs assessment, budget confirmation
- **Tools**: Qualification checklist, discovery call framework
- **Success Metrics**: Qualified opportunity, budget confirmed, decision timeline

**Phase 2: Demonstration (Weeks 3-6)**
- **Activities**: Product demonstration, trial setup, reference calls
- **Tools**: Demo script, trial setup guide, reference program
- **Success Metrics**: Demo completed, trial initiated, positive feedback

**Phase 3: Proposal & Closing (Weeks 7-10)**
- **Activities**: Proposal development, pricing discussion, contract negotiation
- **Tools**: Proposal template, pricing calculator, contract terms
- **Success Metrics**: Proposal accepted, contract signed, payment terms agreed

#### SMB Sales Process (1-3 months)
**Phase 1: Qualification (Week 1)**
- **Activities**: Inbound lead qualification, needs assessment, trial setup
- **Tools**: Lead qualification form, trial setup guide
- **Success Metrics**: Qualified lead, trial initiated, positive engagement

**Phase 2: Demonstration & Trial (Weeks 2-4)**
- **Activities**: Product demonstration, trial management, objection handling
- **Tools**: Demo script, trial management guide, objection handling framework
- **Success Metrics**: Demo completed, trial success, positive feedback

**Phase 3: Closing (Weeks 5-8)**
- **Activities**: Pricing discussion, contract execution, onboarding
- **Tools**: Pricing presentation, contract template, onboarding checklist
- **Success Metrics**: Contract signed, payment processed, onboarding initiated
